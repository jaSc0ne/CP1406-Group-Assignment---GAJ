Mission Statement (Goals)

This website is intended to increase the number of sales of tickets for performances by the Barrier Reef Orchestra and other local musical groups. It is also intended to encourage classical musicians from Townsville and Cairns to join and become members/players of the orchestra. Â 

Success Evaluation

This website will be deemed successful if the total revenue of the Barrier Reef Orchestra is increased by 30% over a period of 12 months. This increase in revenue will be a result from an increased number of ticket sales for performances of the orchestra.
