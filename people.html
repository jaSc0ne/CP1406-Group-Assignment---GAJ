Musicians of the Barrier Reef Orchestra



Guest Musicians


Adam Lopez

Vocalist


Adam Lopez’s exceptional vocal artistry has paved opportunities to perform backing vocals for some of the biggest names in the music industry such as Mariah Carey, Keith Urban and The Coors. His most recent noted collaboration was headlining with Australian Jazz legend James Morrison in a string of Symphonic Concerts in Perth, Hobart and Brisbane.


Domenico Taraborrelli

Accordionist/Vocalist


Domenico is one of Brisbane's most experienced and versatile multicultural cabaret entertainers with the ability to perform an endless repertoire of world music including Jazz, Latin, Ballroom, Background, Rock & Pop and others that are sure to please all.

Guest Conductors


Sean O'Boyle

Australian composer and conductor, Sean O’Boyle, has conducted recordings and concerts with many leading Australian and international orchestras , including the Adelaide, West Australian, Melbourne and Tasmanian Symphony Orchestras, The Queensland Orchestra, the Dortmund Konzert in Germany, the Lexington Philharmonic in the USA, Auckland Philharmonia, BBC Concert Orchestra, Malaysian Philharmonic Orchestra and Orchestra Victoria.


Richard McIntyre

His work as a conductor is well-known, particularly in Canberra. He was Conductor and Musical Director of the Canberra Youth Orchestra from 1980 – 1990, greatly broadening its repertoire and general endeavour, and led it on three highly-successful and widely-acclaimed European tours.
